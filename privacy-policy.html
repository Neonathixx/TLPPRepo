Privacy Policy

At The Little Paw Patissier, we treat your privacy with the same care and high-quality ingredients we use in our artisanal canine confections. Read how we protect you and your furry family members.

Information We Collect How We Use Your Data Pet Data Privacy Cookies & Treats

Information We Collect

When you visit our whimsical online patisserie, we collect certain information to ensure your experience is as delightful as a freshly baked cupcake. This includes:

  • Personal Identification: Your name, email address, address, and phone number when you place an order or sign up for our newsletter.
  • Payment Details: We do not store any credit card information on our server. But we may need proof which includes receipts and invoices.
  • Account Information: Order history, preferences, and communication logs.

We believe in gathering only the ingredients necessary to bake up a perfect customer experience.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is the secret recipe that helps us deliver joy. We use it to:

Fulfill Orders

To process your payments, bake your treats, and deliver them to your doorstep safely.

Improve Services

To analyze website traffic and optimize our menu based on what our furry friends love most.

Pet Data Privacy

Your fur-baby's details are special to us just as they are important to you.

Dogs are our family. When you create a profile for them, you might have to share the following:

  • Their adorable names and nicknames.
  • Breed, age, and weight details.
  • Allergies, dietary restrictions, and favorite flavors.

This information is used strictly to recommend safe, delicious treats and personalize their surprise treats. We will never sell your pet's data to third parties. It stays locked in our database.

Cookies (The Digital Kind)

While The Little Paw Patissier specializes in yummy treats, this does not include digital cookies.

Despite this, we do utilize them to help us remember your preferences, your shopping cart, orders, how you navigate the site, etc. You can choose to opt out of through your settings, but you may be unable to utilize the site's feature in its entirety.

Why Dog-Safe Desserts
Matter

Dogs are a man’s best friend, they aren’t just pets, they are our family, as a family we like to share treats with one another. But human baked treats possess a hidden danger to our little lovable pups, dangers such as sugar, processed grains, artificial coloring, things that can cause harm to our beloved ones. And that’s why The Little Paw Patissier is here, to share these wonderful treats for their celebration while ensuring their safety. Every ingredients matter, just as much as the paws that munch on them.

Baked with passion, served with love.
THE FOUNDER

Meet BLESS & UNO

Bless loves to bake, one day she thought “If I can make delicious snacks for my family and friends, why can’t I do the same for my beloved pup Uno?”, and so with that thought in mind, she started baking for her pups, keeping in mind her fur-baby’s health, she started baking delicious yummy treats for him which then expanded as a business to provide other people with the opportunity to spoil their beloved ones even by a little.

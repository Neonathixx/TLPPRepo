Paws-itive Answers

Everything you need to know about our artisanal, handmade dog treats. If you can't find what you're looking for, our friendly bakers are always here to help.

Ingredients

What ingredients do you use?

We use only human‑grade, dog‑safe ingredients like whole wheat flour, peanut butter (xylitol‑free), pumpkin, and carob. Every ingredient is carefully selected to be both delicious and safe for your pup.

Are your treats allergy-friendly?

Yes! We offer grain‑free and dairy‑free options. Please let us know about any specific allergies when you place your order, and we’ll do our best to accommodate.

Do you use preservatives?

No. Our treats are baked fresh and contain no artificial preservatives. We recommend storing them in an airtight container and consuming within a week for the best freshness.

Ordering

How do I place a custom order?

Simply browse our menu, add items to your cart, and during checkout you’ll be able to leave special instructions or request a custom flavour. We’ll review your request and get back to you within 24 hours.

What is the lead time for cakes?

Custom cakes require at least 3–5 days’ notice. For rush orders, please contact us directly and we’ll see if we can squeeze you in.

Can I cancel my order?

Orders can be cancelled up to 48 hours before the scheduled pickup/delivery date. After that, we’re already busy baking, so cancellation may not be possible. Please reach out and we’ll do our best to help.

Shipping & Delivery

Where do you deliver?

We currently deliver within Metro Manila. Pickup is also available from our shop in Quezon City. We’re working on expanding our delivery area soon!

Process for receiving?

After your order is confirmed, you’ll receive a notification with the pickup/delivery details. For deliveries, our rider will contact you when they’re nearby. Please ensure someone is available to receive the treats.

How are the pastries packaged?

Each item is packed in a sturdy, food‑safe box with a paw‑print ribbon. We take extra care to prevent damage during transport.

Safety

Are these treats safe for puppies?

Absolutely! Our treats are designed for dogs of all ages. However, we always recommend supervising your pup when they’re enjoying any new snack and consulting your vet if you have concerns.

How should I store the pastries?

Keep them in a cool, dry place. Most items stay fresh for up to a week at room temperature. Cakes and pupcakes can be refrigerated for longer shelf life.

Still have questions?

Whether you need a custom flavor profile or just want to say hello, we’re all ears!
